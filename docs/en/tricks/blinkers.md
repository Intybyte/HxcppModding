You are not going to understand fully everything that is going on in a method by just staring at it harder. Solution? 

![Horse Blinkers](../../img/tricks/blinkers.png)

Try to focus on one thing a time, you can go down a function inside the target function you are trying to analyze and focuso on 
that for the time being, and you can do it over and over again, until you can go up and figure out what is actually going on.

Try to not focus on everything, but try giving each function its own deserved time.
